About

Savannah Massey is a poet and artist from Mississippi, though she is currently based in Ohio as a student at Kenyon College. There, she is studying software engineering and applied mathematics. She was an honorable mention in the 2025 Only Poems Poet of the Year Prize, as well as being a YoungArts Award Winner With Distinction in Writing and Scholastic Art & Writing National Medalist. Her work has appeared in the Polyphony Lit, Only Poems, Young Writers Journal and elsewhere. Outside of writing, she enjoys dance, painting and vocal performance.

Eden, Mississippi

On Sundays, we left screen doors that sang as they closed
and went down to the river dividing the bare trees
to baptize each other in the filthy water. We danced
like we were famous and covered our day clothes in mud
because that's what they were for. Once,
we went farther than we were supposed to and found
a starch white carcass stretched across the rocks. You faced
away from me, shirt rolled to your shoulders...
